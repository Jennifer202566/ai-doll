Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2025

Welcome to AI Doll Generator. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our AI Doll Generator service, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Images you upload for transformation into AI doll figures
  • Text prompts you provide to guide the AI generation
  • Technical data including IP address, browser type and version, time zone setting, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and platform
  • Usage data including information about how you use our website and services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To process and complete your image transformations
  • To improve and personalize your experience
  • To develop and improve our services
  • To communicate with you about service-related issues
  • To monitor the usage of our service
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously. Here's how we protect your information:

  • Images you upload are stored temporarily for processing and are automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • Generated images are stored for 7 days to allow you to download them, after which they are automatically deleted
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data
  • All data transfers between your browser and our servers are encrypted using SSL technology

4. Third-Party Services

Our service utilizes the following third-party services:

  • Replicate API - For AI image processing. Your uploaded images are processed through their servers. Please refer to Replicate's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Web hosting providers - For hosting our website and storing temporary data

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access, update, or delete your information
  • The right to rectification (to correct or update your personal data)
  • The right to object (to processing of your personal data)
  • The right of restriction (to request that we restrict processing of your personal data)
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
